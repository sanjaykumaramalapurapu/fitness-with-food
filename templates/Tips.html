Do's and Don'ts

Natural Health and Nutrition Tips

1. Limit sugary drinks

Sugary drinks like sodas, fruit juices, and sweetened teas are the primary source of added sugar,sugar-sweetened beverages increasing risk of heart disease and type 2 diabetes

Healthier alternatives include:

1.water

2.unsweetened teas

3.sparkling water

4.coffee

2.Eat nuts and seeds

Some people avoid nuts because they are high in fat. However, nuts and seeds are incredibly nutritious. They are packed with protein, fiber, and a variety of vitamins and minerals Nuts may help you lose weight and reduce the risk of developing type 2 diabetes and heart disease

3. Avoid ultra-processed foods

1.snack cakes

2.fast food

3.frozen meals

4.packaged cookies

5.chips

4.Eat fatty fish

Fish is a great source of high-quality protein and healthy fat. This is particularly true of fatty fish, such as salmon, which is loaded with anti-inflammatory omega-3 fatty acids and various other nutrients

5.Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables

6.Eat Enought protein

7.Stay hydrated(min 4litres per day)

8.Get enough sleep(6-8hrs per day)
